Welcome to DACCS

We are an independent company based in Tavistock, Devon. We specialise in providing flexible care packages to individuals within our area.

Our clients have many different needs: some full time care while others need short-term support with specific activities.

DACCS is owned by Pauline Hilton and was set up in 2003. Pauline is a registered Care Manager and trained nurse, and holds her professional qualifications.

Her teams of carers are all trained to the standards of the Care Quality Commission (CQC) and attend regular ongoing professional training sessions.

All our carers are checked through the Criminal Records Register, wear uniforms and carry ID for the protection and safety of all clients.
